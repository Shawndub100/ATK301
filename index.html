About Me

My name is Shawn Wimberly. I have been spening the last 10 years of my life mastering techniques to better my music craft. I started drumming when I was 9 years old, but my passion for the piano took over 3 years ago. Shortly after, studying sound became a hobby of mines. My music talents must have just been a gift from God. No one in my family is musicially gifted but me. That made learning music heard because i had no one to depend on to teach me. I eventually begin to teach myself to play by ear. At 20 years old I am still teaching myself the concepts of music on the piano. Eventually I will enroll in classes to teach my how to read music.

My goal is to eventually go out into the world and start my own music company. There I would provide sound, and also music equipment for people that may need it. wheather it be concerts, church events, or just for fun I want to be the guys everyone calls on to get the done

“When I’m singing, it’s like this extra way of connecting and communicating with people beyond what I can do just playing the drums/and organ. I’m able to convey these messages that are really important and meaningful to me through my words. Being front and center like this every night, it’s a challenge, but I’m up for it.”
